Our Story

About Us

Marinduque Pasalubong Hub is a small shop dedicated to sharing the best products from Marinduque. We offer a variety of local delicacies, handmade crafts, buri products, and souvenir items that proudly represent the culture and traditions of the island.

Our goal is to help people bring home something meaningful — whether it's for family, friends, or loved ones. Every product we offer is carefully chosen to make sure it shows the quality, creativity, and heart of Marinduque.

From arrowroot cookies to Moriones souvenirs, Marinduque Pasalubong Hub brings the taste, art, and spirit of the island closer to you.
